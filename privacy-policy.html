Simple Promise: Magic 8 Ball!!! respects your privacy. We don't collect, store, or share your personal information. Your questions and mystical consultations stay private on your device.

Information We Don't Collect

  • We don't collect your questions or responses
  • We don't access your device's contacts, photos, or files

Information Stored Locally

The following information is stored only on your device and is never transmitted elsewhere:

  • Response History: Your previous Magic 8 Ball consultations (stored locally for the history feature)
  • App Preferences: Your chosen theme (light/dark) and language settings
  • Usage Statistics: Basic app usage metrics for ad frequency management (stored locally only)

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Our app displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information for ad personalization and analytics purposes. This is handled entirely by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

What AdMob may collect:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • Ad interaction data
  • General location (country/region level)

You can opt out of personalized ads by adjusting your device's advertising settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising > Personalized Ads (turn off)
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit personal data, there's minimal risk to your privacy. All app data remains on your device and is protected by your device's security measures.

Children's Privacy

Magic 8 Ball!!! is suitable for users of all ages. We don't knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to Privacy Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we'll post the changes on this page and update the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I use Magic 8 Ball!!!?

Simply think of a yes/no question, then shake your device or tap the Magic 8 Ball. Watch as your mystical answer appears!

Q: Can I change the language?

Yes! Go to Settings in the app and select from English, Spanish, French, German, or Japanese.

Q: How do I view my response history?

Tap the "History" button (üìú) on the main screen to see all your previous consultations.

Q: Why am I seeing ads?

Ads help keep Magic 8 Ball!!! free for everyone. We've designed the ad experience to be respectful and not interfere with your mystical consultations.

Q: The app isn't responding to shake gestures

Make sure your device's motion sensors are enabled and try a gentle but firm shake. You can also tap the ball directly if shake isn't working.

Q: How do I switch between light and dark themes?

Open Settings from the main screen and toggle between Light and Dark themes to match your mystical mood.

Troubleshooting

App Performance Issues

  • Force close and restart the app
  • Restart your device
  • Ensure you have the latest version from the App Store
  • Make sure you have sufficient storage space

Shake Detection Not Working

  • Check if motion & fitness permissions are enabled for the app
  • Try a different shake motion (some devices are more sensitive)
  • Use the tap-to-activate alternative
